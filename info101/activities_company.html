HISTORY - FOCUSES

HUMANITY, SUSTAINABILITY, EFFICIENCY

I registered the domain mh-designs.org in August, 2010 in order to ensure the future availability of the brand. The first designs of MH were done at the end of 2010 by Minh HA and Trung TRAN during our summer break. The company however did not have any legal status at that time and the structures and services were designed by the engineers of Thien An Construction.

The company was registered in 2015 as a Limited Company. It was designed following employee-own model to maintain a high level of democracy within the company while keeping the motivation level high. Minh VU joined the company after he finished his Master degree at TUM and Simon took my offer and came to Vietnam to work in a challenging new company and a growing economy.

We designed the prototype of the Weißenhofsiedlung (Weissenhof Estate) at a newly established district of Ho Chi Minh city, Vietnam to attract potential Investors. The name Weißenhofsiedlung was chosen to indicate a strong bond between Germany and the root of our company, the name was an icon of Modernism Architecture and contained in its the ideology of the late Modernist Architects: Appealing Design, Functionability, Affordable and Quick to Construct. What we added was the state of art Sustainable Features.

Our practise focuses on: Sustainable Architectural Design and Engineering, Urban Design, and Project Management.
