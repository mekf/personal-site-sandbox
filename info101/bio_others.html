COMPUTER

I enjoy building the computer that I use from scratch. It's fun because I have complete control on which components would bring the most benefit/cost ratio and be the most suitable for the task that I require.

At the moment I'm using an AMD Phenom II x4 965, 4GB DDR3 RAM, MSI HAWK ATI Radeon 5770, 3TB in RAID5 for storage and 500GB in RAID0 for Operating Systems and Programs. I also enjoy using open source softwares because it provides a degree of freedom that I want. I can modify the program to be tailor fit for what I need.

PHOTOGRAPHY

I enjoy photography because it's a mix between science and art. You need an artist eye to spot beautiful scence and the knowledge about the science of taking picture in order to successfully capture the scence. Fiddling with Aperture, Shutter Speed, ISO speed, White Balance Setting, Picture Style may sound complicated but that's the fun of taking picture with a DSLR.

At the moment I'm using a Canon full-frame 5D MarkII but the lenses that I used were manufactured in 1960s - 1970s. Those lenses are old but the optical quality can be comparable to some of the most expensive lenses these day. The down side is that they require the photographer to be able to manually focus the lenses. I'm using Pentax Super Takumar 55 f1.8, Pentacon 50 f1.8, Ricoh 35 f2.8, and Ricoh 135 f2.8.

MOUNTAIN BIKING

Mountain Biking provides the thrill of living for that moment (sometimes literally). Some says "the journey is more important than the destination", in Mountain Biking there is no destination. It's just you, the bike, your riding buddies, and the trail you ride on.

At the moment, I ride a Transition Bottle Rocket because it fits my style well, a jack of all trade. I do All Mountain Biking, Freeride, Enduro and sometimes Downhill.

MARTIAL ART

I like most martial arts because it unites the mind and the body. I prefer Goju Ryu Karate because it's one of the original style. It focus on the harmonious of the practitioner and nature.
