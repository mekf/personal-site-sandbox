MY PHILOSOPHY

Some may call me a Dreamer, I call myself a Visionary Thinker.
Minh Ha

Try not to become a man of success but rather to become a man of value.
Albert Einstein

This is my simple religion. There is no need for temples, no need for complicated philosophies. My brain and my heart are my temples; my philosophy is kindness.
Dalai Lama

Always bear in mind that your own resolution to succeed is more important than any other.
Abraham Lincoln

Live neither in the past nor in the future, but let each day's work absorb your entire energies, and satisfy your widest ambition.
Sir William Osler

The important thing is not to stop questioning. Curiosity has its own reason for existing. One cannot help but be in awe when he contemplates the mysteries of eternity, of life, of the marvelous structure of reality. It is enough if one tries merely to comprehend a little of this mystery every day. Never lose a holy curiosity.
Albert Einstein

All that is valuable in human society depends upon the opportunity for development accorded the individual.
Albert Einstein

I never teach my pupils. I only attempt to provide the conditions in which they can learn.
Albert Einstein
